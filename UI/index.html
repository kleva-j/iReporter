What is iReporter

This is a platform enables any/every citizen to bring any form of corruption to the notice of appropriate authorities and the general public. You can provide evidence to prove your claim. This kind of evidence can be images or videos. You can also post an address to the site of the incident. This platform also enables every citizen to report issues around their community that require immediate intervention to.

Let us all work together to curb out this epidemic from our society

Steps to take are:

  • Create an account by signing up Here
  • Report incidents of corrupt practices around you by creating a red-flag
  • Reports incidents that need immediate attention by creatin an intervention
  • Attach image or video evidences that will support your report or claims
  • Attach an address of the location/place of incidence
  • Await a resolution to your report
